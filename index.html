Fairfield City School District — Where the Money Goes

School district spending (GAAP — Statement of Activities) · Dollars only
Source & method (GAAP): All “District Spending Total” and category amounts come from Fairfield City School District’s audited Annual Financial Reports, using the government-wide GAAP Statement of Activities (Governmental Activities — Expenses) for each fiscal year shown. Categories reflect audited functional expenses (Instruction, Support Services, Operations & Maintenance, Transportation, Food Service, Extracurricular, and Interest/Fiscal). Items shown as “Included — Not Additional” (benefits and retirement) are embedded within those functional totals and are displayed only for transparency — they are not added again. Reporting titles and line detail can vary slightly by year; totals are anchored to the audited Statement of Activities to prevent double-counting and ensure consistency.
